Why One "Golden Prompt" is Better Than a Thousand Guesses

At PromptVault USA, we believe that AI isn't failing you—your instructions are. Today, I’m pulling back the curtain on our "Vault" to give you a framework that changes everything.

The "S.P.E.C." Framework

  • S - Situation: Who is the AI? (e.g., "You are an expert copywriter.")
  • P - Problem: What is the specific hurdle?
  • E - Expectation: What does the output look like?
  • C - Constraint: What should it avoid?
