01. The Specialized Industry Vault

Instead of general productivity prompts, architect deep-vertical vaults. Real estate agents, lawyers, and e-commerce owners in the US market don't want "writing tips"—they want a "Legal Intake Automation Logic" or a "High-Ticket Listing Narrative Engine." By bundling 10-15 hyper-specialized assets, you can command premium pricing ($97-$297) from professional buyers who value their time.

Revenue Pillar: The "Bundling" Effect

Higher margins are achieved through perceived structural value:

  • The Core Logic: The industrial prompt itself.
  • The Workflow Guide: A PDF explaining how to integrate the prompt into a business.
  • Golden Samples: Visual proof of what the prompt produces.

02. Prompt-as-a-Service (PaaS)

For high-ticket clients, become their internal AI Architect. You don't sell the prompt; you sell the **Result**. This involves creating custom, private vaults for businesses that allow their teams to produce consistent, brand-aligned content. This model shifts your income from one-time sales to high-retainer consulting, establishing you as an essential part of their digital infrastructure.
