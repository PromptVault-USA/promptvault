02. The Three Pillars of Voice Cloning

To successfully clone a voice, you must provide a Style Guide within the prompt. We focus on three critical areas:

  • Cadence: The rhythm of the writing. Is it flowing and academic, or short and punchy?
  • Lexicon: The specific choice of words. Establishing "Forbidden Words" is just as important as choosing "Power Words."
  • Perspective: Is the voice a "Relatable Peer" or an "Authoritative Expert"? This shift changes the entire psychological impact of the content.
