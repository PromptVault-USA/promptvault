Instead of selling generic writing, architects build specialized "Logic Vaults" for businesses. These are complex, multi-step prompt chains that automate a company's specific brand voice or technical documentation. One high-quality vault can be sold as a premium digital asset over and over again.

Small businesses are desperate to use AI but don't know how. You can monetize your expertise by acting as a consultant who integrates sovereign AI workflows into their existing operations—reclaiming their time and charging a premium for the efficiency gain.
